We have a direct democracy. With the three tribes, Domonique tribe, Espanosa tribe, and Shine Tribe. The richest tribe is the Domonique tribe, and the most poor is the Shine Tribe. Each tribe has a representative that voices the peoples decision to the senates in the dome, the senates make the final decisions on the new laws or other government things. The dome is a where the meetings are held,where the people sell their surplus.
