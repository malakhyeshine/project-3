We allow everyone to believe in any relgion that they want, but most of our people are polytheistic, and practice the religion called Ahmadism. This relgion beleives that there are a total of six gods. The Moon God, Lune, The Sun god, Soleil, and the Star goddess, Etolie. The sun god and the star goddess had three children. The land/ Volcano god, volcan, the Ocean god, Eau, and the Nature goddess, Terry. The three children went on to create the planet earth.
